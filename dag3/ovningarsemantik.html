Opening the crib: Frankys

Published on: September 16, 2015

The Burger Gallery will be opening to an invitation-only group of art enthusiasts across the country on December 1, 2013.

The opening of the Burger Gallery will coincide with the release of several of Armando Blontio's finest art pieces, including, "Crème de la Crème," which has a mounting anticipation for its debut.

As a frequently misrespresented artist, Armando Blontio has struggled to uphold his image amidst the criticism. "I hope to dispel the illusion of my self-centeredness, as seen by my critics, with the Burger Gallery opening," said Mr. Blontio.
